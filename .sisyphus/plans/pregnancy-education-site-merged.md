# Pregnancy Education Site MVP - Merged Plan

## TL;DR
> Quick summary: A unified MVP plan combining pregnancy-education site content architecture with evidence governance for credible information, plus a plan for medical review integration, ethics, accessibility, and MVP rollout milestones.

## Context
- Merge of two existing plans:
  - Pregnancy Education Site MVP Plan
  - Evidence Guidance for Pregnancy Education Site
- Objective: deliver a standards-compliant, evidence-based, accessible educational site for pregnant people in Mainland China (Simplified Chinese) with three content phases (孕早期、孕中期、孕晚期) and a robust evidence framework.

## Work Objectives
- Core Objective: Build a content framework and site skeleton for the three pregnancy stages, with evidence-tiered sourcing, ethical considerations, and governance for citations and anonymized case sourcing.
- Concrete Deliverables:
  - 1) Content taxonomy and page templates for three stages
  - 2) Evidence policy, tiering, and citation templates
  - 3) Editorial governance docs (ethics, consent flows, anonymization)
  - 4) Initial MVP site skeleton (navigational structure, search, accessibility)
- Must Have:
  - Content taxonomy document with three pregnancy stages (孕早期/孕中期/孕晚期)
  - Evidence policy document with Tier 1-4 definitions and citation templates
  - Case anonymization workflow with consent tracking
  - Family support module framework
  - Editorial governance & ethics process document
  - MVP site skeleton with navigation, sitemap, and a11y guidelines
  - Launch milestones and monitoring metrics plan
- Must Not Have:
  - No production deployment or live site hosting
  - No external medical consultant contracts (only integration plan)
  - No live user data collection or analytics at MVP stage
  - No payment/e-commerce functionality
  - No content in languages other than Simplified Chinese (MVP scope)
- Estimated Effort: Medium
- Parallel Execution: YES (waves)
- Definition of Done: All deliverables present and auditable

## Verification Strategy (Agent-Executed QA)
- Each core content unit must have a Happy-path and an Edge-case QA scenario
- Evidence sources must be citably traceable
- All tasks must include concrete acceptance criteria and evidence paths

## Execution Strategy
- Wave 1: Foundation – taxonomy, templates, governance scaffold, and site skeleton
- Wave 2: Evidence integration – Tiering, citations, and case workflows
- Wave 3: Editorial governance – ethics, consent flows, anonymization
- Wave 4: Accessibility, SEO, and MVP readiness
- Final Wave: Compliance checks and上线准备
