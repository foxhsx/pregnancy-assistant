# Evidence Guidance for Pregnancy Education Site

## Overview
- This document defines the evidence policy, source hierarchy, citation templates, and governance for all content in the Pregnancy Education Site MVP.

## Evidence Tiering
- Tier 1 (Strongest): World Health Organization (WHO) guidelines, American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG) statements, CDC pregnancy guidance, NICE pregnancy care guidelines, and equivalent high-quality global sources.
- Tier 2 (High quality): Cochrane reviews, high-quality systematic reviews and pivotal RCTs published in top journals (Nature, NEJM, Lancet).
- Tier 3 (Public/Contextual): Public anonymized case summaries, expert consensus with clear limitations, and recently published but evolving studies.
- Tier 4 (Not recommended as primary): Unvetted blogs, tutorials, or non-peer-reviewed sources. (To be avoided as primary citations.)

## Evidence Source List (Draft)
- Tier 1 (Global): WHO, ACOG, CDC, NICE
- Tier 1 (Domestic China): 国家卫健委指南、中华医学会/中国妇产科医师协会等机构发布的权威指南与共识
- Tier 2: Cochrane, Nature/NEJM/Lancet meta-analyses
- Tier 3: 公共公开数据、去识别化的病例汇总、地区性高质量研究的关键结论（需标注局限性）

## Citation Template (示例)
- [Title]. [Source] [Author(s)], [Year]. [DOI/URL]. Last Updated: [YYYY-MM-DD].
- 例： Gestational Diabetes Management. WHO Guidelines, World Health Organization, 2023. https://apps.who.int/gho/.

## Assertion & Sourcing Rules
- Every core fact must be followed by one or more citations.
- If multiple sources exist, provide a concise synthesis and clearly label any conflicts.
- For claims about safety/risks, indicate the strength of evidence (Strong, Moderate, Weak) and risk relevance (High/Medium/Low).

## Governance & Workflow
- Editorial workflow must include a mandatory citation check before publication.
- Internal editor reviews Tier 1/2 sources; external medical consultants added only if feasible in later phases.
- Case sourcing requires strict anonymization and consent tracking for any domestic hospital data; ethical review documentation should be prepared when external data are used.
- Update cadence: formal review every 6-12 months; guideline releases can trigger expedited updates.

## Case Sourcing & Ethics (Guidance)
- Public anonymized cases from open data or published case studies with consent-secured anonymization.
- Domestic hospital cases require ethics approval and data use agreements; ensure privacy protections.
